Option 1:-

SCUBA Diving

Self-Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus

  • It will be 25 - 30 minute long SCUBA Dive fully guided by a Dive Master or SCUBA Instructor (Depth 15 - 40 feet)
  • Pre Dive Briefing, SCUBA Diving Kit, Qualified & Certified Instructors will be Provided


Must Know Things about SCUBA
  • Non Swimmers also can do SCUBA
  • Every participant spends 15-20 minutes underwater on an average
  • Total duration of the activity is 1 hrs
  • Indemnity Bond is compulsory for SCUBA
  • Every weekend Invincible organises SCUBA Diving in 2 half
  • Special Groups above 6 people can organise special SCUBA Diving Session
  • Photos/Videos are shared by Google Drive Only
Note:-
  • The Organisation do not charge anything for underwater videos, that is complimentary.
  • There are chances of technical failure or water damage of the equipment, in that case we won’t be able to share your underwater video.
